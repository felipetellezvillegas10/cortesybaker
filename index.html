CORTES & BAKER LLC




At C&B, we are more than an accounting firm: we are a team that understands each client’s goals and challenges. Our purpose is to accompany them closely, providing clarity, trust, and solutions that drive their growth.

Our story began in 2007, and today we continue to expand with the same conviction: to be a tool for growth for companies and individuals, providing value, trust, and practical solutions that go beyond numbers.

MISIÓN

Mission


We are an accounting firm that supports the growth of our clients and collaborators, working as their strategic partners. We enjoy solving problems in a disruptive and human way, promoting teamwork, communicating clearly, and avoiding unnecessary conflicts—always creating value beyond the numbers.

VISION

Vision


To be recognized as an accounting firm that makes a difference: one that innovates where others do not reach, builds genuine partnerships, always prioritizes people’s well-being, and generates a positive impact on every client, collaborator, and community we work with. We aim to be remembered as a close company that grows alongside others, leaving a legacy of gratitude for the value and impact it created.

ABOUT US

Cortes & Baker was born from the vision of a Mexican entrepreneur with a clear purpose: to transform the way businesses experience accounting.
From the very beginning, we decided to go beyond numbers to become true partners in our clients’ growth.

Today, as we continue to expand across the United States, we remain committed to building genuine relationships and providing tailored solutions that help businesses grow with confidence, strategy, and security.

Our services include Accounting, Payroll, Taxes, Tax Planning, Finance, and Consulting — always with a strategic and human approach.

At Cortes & Baker, we don’t just manage accounts — we drive businesses forward and support people.
